What is Blink?

Blink is an AR comfort companion that brings everyday objects to life. A mug, a shoe, a stuffed animal — photograph it, give it a personality, and it becomes something that talks to you, gets hungry, remembers if you showed up yesterday, and genuinely misses you when you're gone.

It's not an assistant. It's not trying to help. It's just… there. And somehow, that's exactly what you needed.

What Makes It Different

🎭
36 Personalities
Pick two traits from nine — shy + humorous, sassy + warm — and watch a unique character emerge.
🍽️
Feed It
Show it your meals. It has opinions. Skip a day and it gets moody. The weather inside changes.
⏳
Scarcity
Five-minute conversations. Three-hour cooldowns. You can't talk whenever you want — and that's the point.
💚
Growing Bond
Stranger → companion → close friend → soulmate. The relationship changes how it speaks to you.

How It Works

1
Photograph
Take a photo of any object. Blink auto-extracts it from the background.
2
Personalize
Give it a name, pick its eyes and mouth, choose two personality traits.
3
Meet
Your object appears in AR — alive, blinking, and ready to talk.
4
Bond
Feed it, talk to it, come back tomorrow. The relationship grows.
