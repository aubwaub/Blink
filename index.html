How it works

Every object has a story to tell

talkIT turns everyday objects into your personal language teachers. Scan anything around you, and it comes alive to start a conversation in the language you're learning.

📷

Scan & Identify

Point your camera at any object — AI recognizes it and creates an animated sticker teacher with personality.

💬

Bilingual Conversations

Your object speaks your target language, with key vocabulary highlighted. Ask in English anytime — it switches seamlessly.

🎤

Voice & Text

Speak or type — your choice. Hold-to-talk voice input with real-time transcription, or text mode for quieter moments.

🌍

7 Languages

French, Chinese, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, and English — with locale-specific speech recognition.

⭐

4 Difficulty Levels

From kindergarten teacher (beginner) to witty native speaker (advanced) — each with its own personality.

🗂️

Sticker Collection

Every object becomes a collectible sticker saved to your calendar. Review past conversations anytime.
